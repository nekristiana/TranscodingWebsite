Zondere Gezicht Bestaat Men Niet

Seidi Ruder made the protest poster in a time of wide protests
in Netherlands (~1975-1985), which were directly linked to opposition against
first steps of centralized data collection of dutch citizents in a governmental level.


The transcoding project investigates not only the visual history of the time
in Netherlands, but also the connection with the topic of the protest poster in
a contemporary surveillance context of 2020.


The color pixels assembling (dissembling) the image is used to
camoflague the information with the information itself, like Seidi Ruder does
it in his poster (hiding the woman behind the numbers themselves).
